Mozart Vision

The project's objective is the development of a Computer Vision system for the automatic interpretation of guitar sheet music, and converting it into guitar tablature. Uses Hough transform to detect and record the coordinates of the extreme points of horizontal staff lines, providing a reference for pitch placement. Subsequent morphological operations remove staff lines and rectify any resulting image irregularities. Further, image segmentation divides bars into individual notes, while corner detection pinpoints note locations. Centroid analysis links notes to nearby staff lines or spaces, enabling the determination of pitch, fret, and string positions. Lastly, a Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) classifies notes as whole, half, or quarter notes.
